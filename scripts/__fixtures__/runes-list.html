New League - Runes of Aldur

Farrow, a seeker of runes from the eastern reaches of Ezomyr, has uncovered markings that should have remained buried. Each rune he unearths does more than grant power; it reveals fragments of a forgotten bond between Ezomyte tradition and the forbidden craft of the Kalguurans.

With the aid of the Kingsmarch crew, delve into relics long entombed which include ancient treasures, buried truths, and some things best left undisturbed.

Uncover Ezomyte Remnants

Find mysterious Remnants across Wraeclast and awaken their ancient magic by inscribing runic symbols upon them. Depending on which symbols are already present, you’ll have different options for the items you can create using this magic. Be careful, though, as the nearby monsters will be empowered with additional abilities based on the runes you use, making for a much tougher but more rewarding fight.

Break the Remnant and claim the reward you’ve created.

The Power of Verisium

Discover new crafting possibilities using Verisium, an ancient metal used to forge powerful items and unlock a new form of defence, Runic Ward.

Help Farrow master Kalguuran technology to expand your crafting arsenal. Runeforge low-level Uniques, experiment with over a 160+ new runes, and use new types of crafting currency to enhance your equipment in new ways.

  • Runic Alloy
  • Imbued Alloy
  • Expansive Alloy
  • Swift Alloy
  • Mystic Alloy
New Kalguuran Skills

Harness the power of Kalguuran Runecrafting to create Kalguuran Skills. These skills combine ancient runes with corruption-derived gems to create powerful effects, from Triskellion Cascade triggering your next spell multiple times, to Frostflame Nova transforming Ignite into Frostfire.

Kalguuran Skills cost Runic Ward, have no colour, and ignore attribute or weapon requirements, making them usable by any class.

  • Frostflame Nova
  • Runic Infusion
  • Mark of Repulsion
  • Triskelion Cascade
  • Hollow Shell
  • Concussive Runes

The Origins of Divinity

Return of the Ancients

Wraeclast lies in ruin after a devastating cataclysm, consumed by a creeping corruption that gnaws at all it touches. Guided by Doryani, the Vaal thaumaturge, you must seek out the makers of the Third Edict, the Precursors, and uncover what path remains.

Reach the fortress by traversing forsaken maps, and unseal its ancient gates.

Exploring the Fortress

Beyond the walls lies more than first meets the eye. The fortress holds many paths to explore and many new things to find. Power up ancient machines, fight new and deadly foes, discover new depths, and uncover dark secrets buried deep within.

Lose 1 portal per 2 mods on a Waystones, so 6 mod maps should have 3 portals.

New Legends

  • Crystalised Twinning: Essence Packs in Area contain an additional Rare Monster
  • Essence Trove: All Rare monster are Essence monsters
  • Twinned Terrors: Summoning Circles always summon an additional Boss
  • Hunting Grounds: Contains 2 additional Rogue Exiles and 5 additional Rare Beasts
  • Magic Hordes: All Monsters are at least Magic
  • Nature Shrines: Shrines release an Azmeri Spirit when activated
Reshaped Atlas Tree

In Return of the Ancients, we have significantly reworked and upgraded the Atlas Passive Tree. With nodes that combine mechanics in new ways and many providing multiple choices, you can customise your Atlas like never before.

Behold the First Edict

Should you press onward to the fortress’s heart... a mighty foe awaits.

Masters of the Atlas

Embark on a mission with one of three Atlas Masters to pursue unique rewards on the Atlas. Uncover powerful artefacts with Jado, hunt deadly monsters with Hilda, or cleanse the corruption with Doryani.

Specialise Your Atlas

Each completed mission unlocks further abilities and specialisation options, letting you tailor your strategy by choosing different masters and bonuses for each map.

Delirium - The Raven and the Hare

An ancient trickster god has been unleashed, spreading madness across Wraeclast. Do you dare to uncover his origins and confront your darkest fears?

The entity of Delirium, the Strange Voice, has grown in power. A new progression system now measures how far you descend into the fog, with danger and madness intensifying as you venture deeper into the Withered Willow.

Encounter warped reflections of reality alongside nightmarish foes. Those who endure will uncover potent new rewards, as well as deeper, more perilous layers of the Delirium.

Enter the Distortion

Consuming a Strange Fruit draws a pall of fog across the Atlas, its shroud thickening with each step forward. In time, this descent gives rise to a Simulacrum, a twisted echo of one's memory.

Overcome the insanity to earn passage to the Raven Trickster’s domain.

Improved Rewards

Distilled Emotions are powerful Delirium rewards that, in Return of the Ancients, can be used in two ways: to instill notable passive buffs onto amulets, and to craft specific mods onto jewels.

Each emotion can provide modifiers such as critical strike chance, movement speed, and many more valuable stats to jewels.

  • Ancient Liquid Paranoia
  • Potent Liquid Melancholy
  • Concentrated Liquid Suffering
  • Concentrated Liquid Isolation
  • Twisted Amulet

New Liquid Recipes

  • Storm's Rebuke: Fully Broken Armour you inflict also increases Cold and Lightning Damage Taken from Hits
  • Kaom's Blessing: The next Fire Spell you cast yourself using a Warcry is Ancestrally Boosted
  • Augmented Flesh: Grants 2 additional Skill Slots
Face the Madness

After conquering the Simulacrum, you earn the right to stand before Tangmazu. Once the questline is complete, Grand Mirrors will begin to surface across the Atlas. Through them, Tangmazu may be challenged anew, yielding ever more coveted relics to those who tread the path again.

  • The Raven's Flock
  • Horror's Flight
  • Voices

Breach - Waking the Dreamer

The Hiveborn are grotesque creatures from a strange realm, seeking to break free and overrun another. Ailith and the Keepers of the Flame have held them at bay, but their strength wanes as the Hiveborn multiply, forcing Ailith to call upon Exiles to take up the fight.

Annihilate Breaches

Encountering a Breach will now display an indicator, extending the encounter time as enemies within it are slain. Resist the invasion long enough to stabilise the Breach and face the toughest miscreations the Hiveborn have to offer.

Genesis Tree Crafting

Fertilise the tree with Hiveblood harvested from slain Hiveborn abominations to unlock its vast crafting potential. Control what grows upon its branches to access new jewelry base types with powerful modifiers unavailable anywhere else.

Ritual - The Rite of the Nameless

Aoife lingers as a restless spirit, bound to the Wildwood and searching for one who might answer her call. The King in the Mists has claimed her flesh, leaving her soul adrift, untethered and unable to pass beyond. She believes that only by reclaiming what was taken can she be made whole once more.

Foretold Proliferation:

  • Monsters Sacrificed at Ritual Altars grants 25% increased Tribute
  • All Unique Favours at Ritual Altars will instead be Currency
  • Rerolling Favours at Ritual Altars has no Cost the first time

Foretold Bounty:

  • 5 Chaos Orbs
  • Mageblood
  • Omen of Greater Annulment
Meet the King

Head into the Wildwood to complete Rituals that resurrect bound monsters, earning Tribute that can now be spent on Uniques or Omens, making these encounters more rewarding than before.

Use any remaining Tribute to fill the meter and gain an audience with the King in the Mists.

Become the Champion

The Rite of the Nameless begins as a ritual encounter, swelling in power until the Map Boss is defeated, leaving behind a fragment of an effigy.

It then bleeds into other maps, with each choice you make compounding the difficulty and rewards. Fight your way through and complete it to face a devastating final trial.

Challenge the Darkness

With the Rite of the Nameless brought to its end, etch your name into the effigy and step into the prison, where the darkness itself awaits.

  • Sylvan's Effigy
  • Liminal Coil
  • Periphery

Abyss

When you first encounter one in the endgame, you'll get a quest that leads you to the east, where you will find these mega abyss cracks on the earth, leading away from the Well of Souls. All the maps along the cracks will contain abysses with special properties, and mapping along these cracks will close the Atlas scale abysses, giving you points for the Abyss Atlas Tree.

At the end of each Atlas Abyss, you'll find an abyssal depths with the boss fight for one of the Abyssal factions. After completing all three, you'll get a key for the Well of Souls, allowing you fight the Vessel of Kulemak.

New Ascendancy

New Huntress Ascendancy - The Spirit Walker

Bound to the wilds, this class calls upon spirits to answer to her will granting different abilities aligning with the Stag, Owl or Bear. Through their guidance, the Spirit Walker may subdue even the most formidable beasts, binding them in spirit, or call forth a spectral companion to fight beside her.

New Monk Ascendancy - The Martial Artist

After decades of training, the Martial Artist has become a master of illusions. Empowered by the runes socketed into his body, this class creates powerful illusions, summons illusory bells to aid him, and excels at using his hands as weapons.

and Much More!

Over 30 New Maps

As part of the endgame overhaul, there are over thirty new areas to explore on the Atlas. From the deserts of Maraketh and snowbound towns to lush gardens and lava-filled dungeons, each map features a distinct environment and a boss to defeat.

40+ New Unique Items

Return of the Ancients introduces over 30 new Unique Items, featuring powerful skills and distinctive mechanics. We have also revised more than a dozen existing Unique Items.

Introducing Challenges

In the Runes of Aldur league, we're introducing the very first set of eight league challenges to Path of Exile 2. Completing them will grant you pieces of the exclusive Knight of Aldur Armour Set.

Streamlining the Campaign

The campaign has been updated to make navigating areas easier. Look out for cues that will guide you forward!

Build Planner

As many players choose to follow build guides from content creators, we’re introducing a Build Guide system to make this process easier. Simply download the build file you’d like to use, and it will appear in the game client, showing everything needed to create that build, including Passive Tree nodes, Ascendancy points, Skill Gems, and more.
